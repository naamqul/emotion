Emotion Recognition

methodology: multi-task transfer learning approach to predict valence [-1,1], arousal [-1, 1], and 8 emotion labels.

architecture + data: efficientNetB0 base pretrained on imagenet with 2 fully-connected layers and 3 output heads trained and finetuned on AffectNet8 (~290,000 Images).

everything is running client-side in browser so no data is collected. opencv used for bounding boxes (haar cascade)
